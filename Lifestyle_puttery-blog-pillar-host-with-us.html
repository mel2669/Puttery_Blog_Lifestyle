Group Events & Private Parties at Puttery

Skip the conference room. Puttery is a refined putting lounge built for gatherings that feel intentional — corporate offsites, client dinners, milestone celebrations, and brand activations people reference afterward.

Our events team works with you on capacity, food and beverage, AV needs, and flow so the night runs itself while your guests stay present. Whether you need a semi-private suite for twenty or a full-venue buyout for two hundred, every location delivers moody interiors, chef-driven menus, and competitive energy without the kitsch.

Custom proposals cover staffing, dietary accommodations, branded touchpoints, and timeline — so planners spend less time chasing vendors and more time on the guest list. Browse city-specific guides below for capacity notes, local menu highlights, and lead times. When you're ready, inquire about events and our team will follow up with options tailored to your market.
